Our Story

Spreading Smile Charity, Non-Profit Organization

ASALAAM ALEIKUM BROTHERS & SISTERS 💗 SPREADING SMILE ORGANIZATION is a non-profit charity organization that has been opened to create a lasting solution to poverty, hunger and social injustice by helping the helpless/needy, the orphans and the ones whos rights have been snatched off as we believe each and every individual & families in our community and around the world has a right to food, shelter, clothing, education and most importantly a happy smile plastered on their faces.

Our Mission

WE FOR YOU to work with communities and serve individuals and families in need & care for the earth. To achieve our aim and goals, each and every member of S.S.O will contribute and everything counts as long as we all cooperate and work together with smiles spread across our faces ☺ By the end of months we try to help feeding the orphans and the poor with what we have contributed and visit them, cook for them and have fun with them We will also have more programs like visiting the sick in the hospital, cleaning mosques and participating in street cleaning and feeding etc!!!

Clothing donation to urban area

Last month, our team orchestrated a meaningful initiative to donate clothes in Kenya. Recognizing the pressing need for assistance in certain communities, we collected a diverse range of clothing items through a collaborative effort. Partnering with local organizations, we ensured the efficient distribution of these donations to individuals and families in need. Witnessing the gratitude and smiles on the faces of those who received the clothes reinforced the impact that even small acts of kindness can have on communities. This experience not only provided essential resources but also fostered a sense of solidarity and shared humanity, inspiring us to continue making a positive difference in the lives of others.
